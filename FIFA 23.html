What is EA SPORTS™ FIFA 23?

EA SPORTS™ FIFA 23 brings The World’s Game to the pitch, with both men’s and

women’s FIFA World Cup™ tournaments, the addition of women’s club teams and new

ways to play your favorite modes. Advances in HyperMotion2 Technology bring

even more of the action and realism of football to the pitch on PS5. Driven by

twice as much real-world motion capture, there is now more true football animation

than ever before in every match.


Gameplay

Experience groundbreaking next-gen HyperMotion gameplay technology* and enjoy

cross-play on same-generation consoles. FIFA 23 also features a new risk-versus-reward

shooting mechanic in addition to refreshed free kicks, penalties and corners.


Women's Club Football

Play as your favorite women’s club in FIFA 23 with your choice of some of the biggest

teams and leagues in the world — the Barclays Women’s Super League, Division 1 Arkema,

the National Women’s Soccer League and the UEFA Women’s Champions League.
